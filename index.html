Dalin Guo

Bio

I am a Research Engineer at Google DeepMind Applied. I was a Machine Learning Engineer at Twitter Cortex, working on recommender system research.

Prior to that, I am a PhD candidate in Cognitive Science at UC San Diego working with Prof. Angela Yu. I received B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2016, and master degree in Intelligent Systems, Robotics, and Control from UC San Diego in 2018.

I was a Software Engineer and Machine Learning intern at Facebook working on news feed recommendation during summer 2020. I was a Machine Learning Researcher intern at Twitter Cortex London office working on deep reinforcement learning for online recommendation during summer 2019.

Research Interest

My research interest lies in machine learning and cognitive science. For my PhD research, I use computational methods to understand reward-based human learning and decision-making under uncertainty. My PhD thesis is on modeling human behavior in multi-armed bandit tasks, where an agent chooses among novel options, trading-off between exploration and exploitation.
